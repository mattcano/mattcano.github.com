My Icon

My icon is an evolution of a sketch that I made sometime in middle school (probably to pass time while bored in class). The icon is primarily made of the four letters of my name: "M", "A", "T" and "T". Do you see it?

The icon has since taken on an extra special meaning. On October 22, 2007, the cabin that my Dad and Grandpa built with their own hands, the place where I created many amazing memories with my family and friends, burnt to the ground in one of a series of wildfires that devastated Southern California that year. The smoke and smokestack on the icon is my ode to the Cano family cabin that I hope to rebuild some day.

I'll be the first to admit that the icon is not the most asthetically pleasing, so I thank you for humoring me up to this point. At the very least, creating the icon was a fun exercise in learning Adobe Illustrator
